22 Beach Stories

22 Beach Stories is a gift a friend for his 22nd birthday.

The original idea was do design a logo including "22". A few days before I played GTA with my brother so this idea came out.

There are five differents lightings corresponding to the differents states of the day. The late night one is my favorite tho.

The foam of the waves are proceduraly generated according to the ditance from the shore. This was the most difficut to achieve. The rest was kinda done in a rush so the characters are also very simple. I tryed to cache the silouhette of the palm tree, even if they look quite simple.

I often have the impression on such scenes with large perspective that the few area that the background takes are the most important. You may have to just one a palm tree in front of the camera that takes the half of the screen but if the buildings in the background miss, the scene feeles easely unbalanced.
